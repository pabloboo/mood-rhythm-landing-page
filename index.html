Frequently asked questions

How much does MoodRhythm cost?

MoodRhythm is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees or in-app purchases. We believe in making emotional well-being tools accessible to everyone.

Is MoodRhythm available on Android and iOS?

No, MoodRhythm is currently only available on Android devices. We may work in the future on an iOS version and will keep you updated on our progress. Sign up for our waitlist to stay in the loop.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. At MoodRhythm, we prioritize your privacy and security. All your data is stored securely in your device and is never shared with third parties. You are always in control of your information, and we adhere to strict data protection standards. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
